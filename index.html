Welcome to The Tower of Henoi

The goal of the game is to move all of the disks off of the first pole one at a time and onto either the second or third post in order. The only caveat is that you may only stack a disk on top of another if it is smaller then the one below it. To move a disk, click on it, drag your mouse so that your cursor is positioned over a pole, and let go to watch the disk fall to the bottom. Have fun and good luck!

If you'd like to play again, click the play again button, otherwise click the end button to be taken back to the main screen
